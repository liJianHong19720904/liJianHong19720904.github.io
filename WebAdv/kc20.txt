2.introduction
   在本课程结束前，特别在这里设计一个总结篇。既综合应用前面的程序设计技巧，又以项目需求为驱动，展示了前端Web应用软件开发的一个过程。与之前的项目开发篇的对比，本课的8个过程案例更为精炼，逐步展示实现了一个图文、音频丰富、有较好交互效果的项目。
   本项目以经典名著《傲慢与偏见》的原文朗读音频互动流畅播放为目标，由于这个36个音频资源存放在gitHub免费Web服务器上（在国内加载速度奇慢，且稳定性差），URL地址：https://lijianhong19720904.github.io/images/mp3/pride and prejudice(01--36).mp3，而这个极低网速的软件运行环境，却正好成为我们学习实践Web前端媒体事件响应的平台，促使我们编写更高质量的代码。
   技术上，本例借助远程低速网络，介绍了媒体对象的属性（以音频为例），了解媒体对象的信息和异步事件、探索媒体编程控制。
   本课程也可以看作开发篇的续作，完成后可以掌握Web页的音视频控制，配合开发篇的“CET项目”中比较系统完整的字符文字展示项目，基本综合了有关Web平台的多媒体图文的编程和探索，开发者总结这二个项目，特别是大学生身份的开发者，结合自身的需求，未来必将可以写出“功能更加强大，效果更丰富，适应环境能力更好”的英语学习软件应用项目。

“Pride and prejudice” Chapter 1
It is a truth universally acknowledged, that a single man in possession of a good fortune must be in want of a wife. However little known the feelings or views of such a man may be on his first entering a neighbourhood, this truth is so well fixed in the minds of the surrounding families, that he is considered as the rightful property of some one or other of their daughters. 
Mr. Bennet was so odd a mixture of quick parts, sarcastic humour, reserve, and caprice, that the experience of three and twenty years had been insufficient to make his wife understand his character. Her mind was less difficult to develope. She was a woman of mean understanding, little information, and uncertain temper. When she was discontented, she fancied herself nervous. The business of her life was to get her daughters married; its solace was visiting and news. 
“Pride and prejudice”  Chapter 2
Mr. Bennet was among the earliest of those who waited on Mr. Bingley. He had always intended to visit him, though to the last always assuring his wife that he should not go; and till the evening after the visit was paid, she had no knowledge of it. It was then disclosed in the following manner. Observing his second daughter employed in trimming a hat, he suddenly addressed her with, "I hope Mr. Bingley will like it, Lizzy."
The rest of the evening was spent in conjecturing how soon he would return Mr. Bennet's visit, and determining when they should ask him to dinner.
